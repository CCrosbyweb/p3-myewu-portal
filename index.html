Jobs, Internships & Careers

Will you have a job when you graduate? We're glad you asked. EWU excels at helping students find opportunities during college and after graduation, and EWU's Career Services continues to grow in order to provide you with more services, tools and strategies to land your first job after graduation. All new students should set up an appointment with Career Services to get on the right track to career succes.

Student Activities

When you talk about opportunity at EWU, you're not just talking about jobs or internships at exciting companies--although you can find plenty of those. You're talking about clubs, volunteering, and leadership you don't get at other colleges or universities. With more than 130 clubs, a new office of community engagement, and plenty of chances to gain leadership experience, EWU lets you find a way to stand out and fit in at the same time.
